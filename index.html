ABOUT

Located in Lilongwe’s affluent Area 43 neighborhood, Ufulu Gardens is a 4–star, up-scale hotel and conference center renowned for its meticulously groomed landscape, friendly and attentive staff, and its quiet, relaxing atmosphere. Centrally situated between Kamuzu International Airport and City Center, Ufulu Gardens Hotel is conveniently close to bars and restaurants, shopping centers, and a short drive to the beautiful Lake Malawi.

Whether for accommodation, conferences, weddings or other functions, Ufulu Gardens Hotel is the ideal destination that transforms ordinary stays and events into special occasions.
